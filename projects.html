Vehicle Parking Application


The Vehicle parking app is multiuser app which requires one admin and other users/ customers. It acts as platform for providing comprehensive four-wheeler parking servicing and solutions. The admin creates and manages the parking lots, parking spots. Customers can quickly check available parking spots based on their locations and can book and occupy the parking spots accordingly. Once the parking service is completed customer can release the parking spot. Admin and customer can see parking statistics on their respective dashboards for the revenue and usages.
